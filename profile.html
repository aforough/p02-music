Kathryn Cain

Performance pianist


Born in Vienna, Kathryn is an accomplished, classically-trained performance pianist. She studied at the University of Music and Performing Arts, with a focus on composition. Her repertoire ranges from Baroque to Contemporary, but Kathryn has a keen interest in pieces composed by Chopin, Rachmaninoff, and Lizst. She has performed in many concerts and recitals around world, and has won awards for them.
